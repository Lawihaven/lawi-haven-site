Experiences

Surf & Waves

Siargao is the surfing capital of the Philippines and our front yard puts you within paddling distance of mellow beginner waves and world‑class reef breaks. Wake up early for sunrise sessions or spend your evenings watching the sunset from the shore. We work with trusted local coaches and can arrange private or group lessons, as well as board rentals for all levels.

Cook & Connect

Community is at the heart of Lawi Haven Stay. Our open‑air kitchen and dining space invite guests to prepare and share meals using fresh local produce. Join our communal dinners, learn Filipino recipes from our hosts and connect with fellow travellers over delicious food. Special requests like cooking classes or private dinners can be arranged on request.

Slow Living

Slow down and reconnect with yourself. From weekly beach jams and sunset markets to sunrise yoga and journaling sessions, Lawi encourages you to embrace island rhythms. Explore Burgos by bicycle, read in a hammock or simply watch the coconut palms sway. Earplugs are provided for the occasional karaoke night next door so you can rest easy.
