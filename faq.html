Frequently Asked Questions

What are your check‑in and check‑out times?

Check‑in begins at 2:00 PM and check‑out is by 11:00 AM. If you arrive early or would like a late check‑out, please contact us and we will do our best to accommodate your request.

Do you offer free Wi‑Fi?

Yes. Complimentary high‑speed Wi‑Fi is available throughout the property so you can stay connected while enjoying island life.

Can you arrange airport or ferry transfers?

Absolutely. We can organise a van, tuktuk or motorbike to pick you up from Sayak (Siargao) airport or the ferry terminal. Please book your transfer on our Services page or reach out via WhatsApp in advance.

Is surfboard rental or coaching available?

Yes. We partner with local surf schools to provide lessons and board rentals for all levels. Visit our Services page to learn more and make a request.

How close are you to major attractions?

Our homestay is centrally located in Burgos. Nearby attractions include Alegria Beach (2.3 miles), General Luna Beach (1.1 miles) and Tak Tak Falls (2.1 miles)【488069385298067†L240-L245】. Surf breaks, cafés and markets are just a short walk away.

Do you provide earplugs?

Yes. Burgos can be lively at times with church bells and karaoke. We offer complimentary earplugs at check‑in to ensure a restful night’s sleep.
