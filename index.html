Welcome to Lawi

Lawi – Live Always With Intention is our modern yet soulfully raw island escape in Burgos, Siargao’s quiet north. Crafted by two friends who believe in slow living and authentic travel, our homestay blends local wood artistry with clean modern design. We sit directly on Siargao Circumferential Road, just steps from surf breaks, a quiet beach and local eateries. The property features two thoughtfully designed private rooms and a spacious ten‑bed dormitory, all furnished with natural materials, mosquito nets and ambient lighting. Warm 24‑hour reception care, eco‑friendly touches and a centred location make Lawi an honest and connected experience of island life.

Our Spaces

Private Rooms

Two intimate rooms featuring handcrafted wood furniture, cozy double beds, mosquito nets and ambient lighting. Guests share nearby bathrooms and enjoy easy access to the beach. Ideal for couples or solo travellers seeking a restful retreat.

Shared Dormitory

A spacious ten‑bed dorm with four single bunk beds, two cozy doubles for couples or comfort seekers, and two single beds in a semi‑private nook. Each bed comes with a mosquito net and personal light. A welcoming space to meet fellow travellers.

Amenities & Location

Beachfront on Siargao Circumferential Road with free parking and 24‑hour reception. Fast wifi, communal areas and thoughtful touches like earplugs at check‑in ensure a comfortable stay. Steps from surf breaks, cafes, markets and Sunday beach jams.
