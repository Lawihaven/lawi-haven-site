About Lawi

Lawi Haven Stay is an eco‑luxury accommodation on Siargao Island. Our philosophy is about living intentionally and connecting with nature. We blend sustainability with comfort, using natural materials like raffia, bamboo and driftwood in our design. Guests can enjoy a communal kitchen, cozy spaces, and a relaxed surfing lifestyle.
